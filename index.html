Welcome to CodeWare IT

At Codeware IT, we combine years of software industry experience with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between what students learn and what companies need.

Based in Dehradun, we offer practical training in MERN Stack and industry tools, preparing you for real-world challenges. Join us to build the skills that top companies are looking for!

MERN Full Stack

MERN is a full-stack JavaScript framework comprising MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js.

It enables developers to build scalable, modern web applications with a unified language (JavaScript) across both frontend and backend.
